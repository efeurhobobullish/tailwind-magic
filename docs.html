Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to convert your UI screenshots to Tailwind CSS code:

  1. Upload a clear screenshot of your UI design
  2. Click the "Generate" button
  3. Wait for our AI to analyze your design
  4. Copy the generated Tailwind CSS code
  5. Paste it into your project and customize as needed

Best Practices

Image Quality

Use high-resolution screenshots for best results. Blurry images may lead to inaccurate code generation.

Simple Layouts

Our AI works best with clean, well-structured UIs. Complex designs may require manual adjustments.

Customization

The generated code is a starting point. You'll likely want to tweak colors, spacing, and other details.
